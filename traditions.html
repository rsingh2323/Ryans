Popular Christmas Traditions

Christmas is celebrated in many different ways across the globe. Here are some popular traditions:

  • Christmas Markets: Common in Europe, these markets sell festive goods, decorations, and food.
  • Secret Santa: A popular gift exchange where participants draw names anonymously.
  • Nativity Scenes: Many display nativity scenes to depict the birth of Jesus.
  • Yule Log: In some cultures, a large log is burned in the fireplace to celebrate the season.
